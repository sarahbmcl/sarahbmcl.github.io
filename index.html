About Me

No--This isn't an SPCA commercial.

I moved to Austin, TX in 2010 to begin my college career, and graduated from the University of Texas at Austin with both my bachelor's and master's degree in Social Work. Following this public service path, I gained my full clinical licensure and have been practicing therapy at a hospital for children. While this work is both rewarding and challenging, I transitioning from the healthcare field into the tech field to pursure more creative endeavors in Web Development.

I am currently completing my Web Development Certification from the University of Texas Coding Boot Camp, where I am focusing on both front-end and back-end web development. I focus on efficiency, communication, creativity, and collaboration. My goal is to expand my current skill set to create a lifelong career path in the tech industry serving clients and my organization.
